# iDeals Tech Hub

An all-in-one business management command centre built specifically for gadget and electronics retailers — shops that sell devices, do repairs, accept trade-ins, and manage customer relationships. Everything a store needs to run is in one system, accessible by different staff members based on their role.

---

## Overview

iDeals Tech Hub replaces the combination of spreadsheets, paper repair logs, WhatsApp threads, and disconnected tools that most gadget shops rely on. It brings sales, inventory, leads, repairs, payments, customer management, marketing, reporting, and operations into a single platform with role-based access — so every staff member sees exactly what they need to do their job and nothing more.

The system has two layers:
- An **internal staff workspace** accessible after login, with 26 modules organized into 6 sidebar groups
- A **public storefront** customers can browse without logging in

---

## Current Modules

The sidebar organizes all modules into six groups. Here is what each one currently does.

---

### CORE

**Dashboard**
The first screen on login. Shows today's business at a glance — total revenue, active leads, stock alerts, and pending payments with trend indicators. Includes a live activity feed of recent sales and events, a revenue chart, top-performing products, quick action buttons (Create Quote, Add Inventory, Log Payment, New Lead), and an AI panel that surfaces trends and recommendations automatically.

**Analytics**
Deep performance data across five tabs. Overview shows revenue vs monthly target, sales funnel from leads through to completed sales, top products by revenue, customer segments, and repair revenue. Revenue breaks down each month with orders, average order value, and target tracking. Customers shows repeat rate, churn rate, NPS score, and individual team member conversion performance. Operations covers repair throughput and team performance summaries.

**AI Studio**
A conversational AI assistant for the business. Ask it questions about performance, get pricing or stock recommendations, or request written summaries of what the data is telling you.

---

### COMMERCE

**POS (Point of Sale)**
The checkout counter for in-store sales. Search products by name, brand, price range, or IMEI. Build a cart with quantity adjustments, discounts, and IMEI capture per item. Supports trade-in deductions at checkout and installment plan setup. Generates a receipt on completion. Includes an Intelligence tab showing demand forecasts, slow-moving stock, bundle suggestions, anomaly alerts, and peak hour analysis running in the background to help staff sell smarter.

**Inventory**
The complete product catalogue. Every item has its name, category, condition grade (New, Used — Excellent, Used — Good, Refurbished), price, stock quantity, shelf location, supplier, colour, and IMEI or serial number. The global search bar in the top navigation searches across inventory by name, IMEI, or colour and navigates directly to that product. Filters by condition, low stock, and out-of-stock. New products are added via the Add Product modal.

**Leads**
A Kanban pipeline with three columns — Hot, Warm, Cold. Every potential customer goes here with their name, phone, source channel (WhatsApp, Instagram, Walk-in, Referral, SMS, TikTok), product interest, budget, assigned staff member, and last contact time. Hovering a lead card reveals quick actions: call, open WhatsApp, or advance to the next stage. The + button on each column adds a lead directly into that temperature. Clicking a card opens a full detail panel.

**Sales**
Two functions on one page. The left side has a product catalogue for building a cart with customer name, delivery method, and payment method selection. The right side is a cart panel that totals the order and lets staff either generate a PDF quote or complete the sale — which records it instantly in the recent sales table. The table shows every sale with order ID, customer, items, total, payment method, status, and date.

**Payments**
A master-detail transaction view. The left panel lists all incoming transactions with filter pills (All, Verified, Pending, Needs Review, Failed) and a search bar. Clicking a transaction opens the full detail on the right — reference number, customer, payment method, amount, status, line items with individual prices, the total, and a context-aware action button. Pending transactions show a "Mark as Verified" button. Verified transactions show print and refund options.

**Customers**
Every customer the business has served, shown as profile cards with segment badge (New, Repeat, VIP, At-Risk), lifetime value, total orders, and first purchase date. Filterable by segment. Clicking a card opens a full customer profile with order history, average order value, repair count, active warranties, and contact details. The New Customer button adds a customer directly.

**Repairs**
The repair job queue displayed as cards. Each card shows the repair ID, device, issue, current status, assigned technician, ETA, and cost estimate. Filterable by status. Clicking a card opens a detail panel with the complete workflow timeline — Received → Diagnosed → Parts Pending → In Progress → Ready → Collected. The action button advances the job to the next stage with one click. Technicians can add notes at any stage. Parts are tracked individually with their own status (Pending, Ordered, Installed). When the device is ready, a Notify Customer button sends the update.

**Warranty**
Two tabs. Warranties lists every active warranty with customer name, device, IMEI, warranty type, expiry date, and days remaining. Filterable by status (Active, Expiring Soon, Expired). Returns & Refunds lists all return requests — staff can approve or reject pending ones, and the system tracks refund amounts and outcomes.

### INTELLIGENCE

**Price Intel**
Monitors your selling prices against the market. Each product shows your current price, the market average, the difference between them, and a demand score. Alerts are categorised as High (action needed), Medium (monitor), or Opportunity (room to charge more). Alerts can be dismissed once reviewed. Clicking a product opens a competitor comparison breakdown.

**Trade-In**
A four-step calculator for staff or customers at the counter. Select brand → model → storage size → condition, and it instantly calculates the trade-in offer. Shows upgrade options the value can go towards. Lets you book an appointment or send the offer via WhatsApp. The right panel shows recent trade-ins this month and the most frequently traded devices.

**Delivery**
Tracks all outgoing deliveries. Each order shows the customer, priority (High, Medium, Low), status (Pending, In Transit, Delivered), delivery address, items ordered, assigned driver, zone, and ETA. Filterable by status so staff can see at a glance what still needs to go out.

---

### FINANCE

**Wallet**
A store-credit wallet system for customers. Four balance tiers — Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum — each with different loyalty bonuses. Staff search customers by tier or name, view the current balance, and top up the wallet. Each customer's wallet shows a full transaction history of deposits and spend.

**Expenses**
Full profit and loss tracking. The Overview tab shows monthly revenue, total expenses, and gross profit with a comparison chart. Each expense category (Rent, Staff, Marketing, Utilities, etc.) shows spend against budget with an On Track, Near Limit, or Over Budget indicator. A YTD banner shows year-to-date revenue, profit, and expenses. The Transactions tab is a log of every income and expense entry. The Budgets tab shows all categories as progress bar cards.

**Suppliers**
Two tabs. Suppliers shows all supplier contacts with name, category, star rating, phone number, lead time, payment terms, and total order value. Purchase Orders lists every order placed — status (Pending, In Transit, Delivered, Cancelled), items, order date, expected delivery, and value. Clicking an order opens a detail panel. A New Purchase Order button creates an order directly from a supplier's card.

**Reports**
Generates exportable reports. Eight report types: Sales, Inventory, Customer, Repairs, Profit & Loss, Team Performance, Marketing, and Loyalty. Select report type, set a date range, choose the format (PDF, Excel, or CSV), and tick which sections to include. The system generates the file and offers download or email delivery. A report history tab stores all previously generated reports.

**Marketing**
Campaign management across four tabs. Overview shows all active campaigns with their channel, status, spend, and ROI. Analytics is a table showing every campaign's budget, spend, revenue generated, ROI, lead count, cost per lead, and conversion rate. Campaigns manages the campaign list and creative performance. Calendar shows upcoming campaigns on a monthly view with a sidebar of scheduled events by channel.

---

### GROWTH

**Loyalty**
The store's customer rewards programme. Four tiers: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum. Members tab lists all enrolled customers with tier, points balance, lifetime spend, visit count, and progress to their next milestone. Tiers tab shows each tier's benefits. Rewards tab is the redemption catalogue for point exchanges. Analytics tab shows points issued vs redeemed, retention rate, and overall programme performance.

**Calendar**
Schedules and displays store events, customer appointments, repair pickups, follow-up calls, and campaign launch dates. Staff add new events via the Add Event modal with type, date, time, and notes.

---

### OPERATIONS

**Authentication**
Issues official authenticity certificates for devices the store sells. Each certificate is tied to a device's IMEI, condition grade, and warranty expiry. The IMEI Checker tab checks a serial number against known stolen or blocked device records. The Generate Certificate tab creates a new certificate for a freshly sold or assessed device. Stats show certificates issued, IMEI checks run today, and the clean device rate.

**Team**
Staff directory showing every team member with their role, contact details, and performance snapshot. Used by managers to see who is active and how each person is tracking.

**Users**
Creates and manages login accounts for staff. Each account is assigned one of five roles — Admin, Sales Manager, Sales Rep, Technician, or Inventory Manager — which automatically controls which modules that person can access and use.

**Settings**
Seven sections in one place:
- **Branding** — Business name, tagline, phone number, WhatsApp number, address, and primary colour
- **Templates** — 8 editable WhatsApp/SMS message templates: Quote Ready, Repair Complete, Payment Reminder, Delivery Update, Trade-In Offer, Birthday Reward, Warranty Expiry, New Arrival — each with variable fields
- **Automation** — 8 active rules that trigger messages or alerts automatically: lead follow-up reminders, low stock alerts, payment overdue notifications, repair completion notices, warranty expiry warnings, and birthday messages
- **Integrations** — Connection status and last sync time for WhatsApp Business API, MTN MoMo, DHL, QuickBooks, Google Analytics, Instagram, TikTok, and Mailchimp
- **Team & Roles** — Role definitions, permissions list, and member counts per role
- **Security** — Password policy, two-factor authentication, and session management

---

### PUBLIC

**Storefront**
A customer-facing shop page accessible without logging in. Displays the full product catalogue with photos, prices, condition grades, and specifications. Customers can browse, open individual product detail pages, and add items to a cart for enquiry or purchase.

## Business Benefits

**Single source of truth.** Every sale, repair, payment, customer, and stock item lives in one system connected to the same database. No more cross-referencing WhatsApp, a spreadsheet, and a paper ledger.

**Real-time financial visibility.** Revenue, expenses, gross profit, and margin are always current. The P&L view and monthly breakdown mean you know exactly where the business stands at any point.

**Customer intelligence.** Every customer has a trackable lifetime value. You know who your VIPs are, who is at risk of leaving, and who has not ordered recently — so you can act on it.

**Stock never runs out silently.** Low stock and out-of-stock alerts are visible on the dashboard and inventory page. The POS Intelligence Panel predicts demand and suggests reorder quantities before you run out.

**Repair jobs never fall through the cracks.** Every repair has a status, a technician, an ETA, and a note trail. The system tells you exactly when to notify the customer. Nothing gets forgotten.

**Pricing confidence.** Price Intelligence monitors competitor prices and tells you when you are underpriced (leaving money on the table) or overpriced (losing sales).

**Marketing ROI is measurable.** Every campaign shows budget, spend, revenue generated, ROI, leads, cost per lead, and conversion rate — so you know which channels are actually paying off.

**Staff accountability without micromanagement.** Each staff member only sees the modules for their role. A sales rep cannot access expenses or user management. A technician cannot edit sales records.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React 19 + TypeScript
- **Build Tool:** Vite
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS (with class-based dark mode)
- **Routing:** React Router v7
- **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL + Edge Functions)
- **Icons:** Remix Icons
- **State:** React Hooks with optimistic updates and Supabase fallback

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Node.js 18+ and npm

### Installation

```bash
git clone https://github.com/oscarkay99/ideals_gh.git
cd ideals_gh
npm install
npm run dev
```

The app runs on `http://localhost:5173` by default.

### Supabase

Create a `.env.local` file:

```bash
VITE_SUPABASE_URL=your-project-url
VITE_SUPABASE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY=your-publishable-key
```

Apply the database schema by running the migration files in `supabase/migrations/` through the Supabase SQL editor or CLI.

### Production Build

```bash
npm run build
```

```
│   ├── base/              # Reusable UI components (Button, Input, StatCard)

├── types/                 # TypeScript interfaces
├── router/                # Route configuration
└── index.css              # Global styles and dark mode variables
supabase/
├── functions/             # Edge functions (pos-ai, learn-patterns, weekly-insights)
└── migrations/            # SQL schema files
```
